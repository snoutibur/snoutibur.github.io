A remix of Sad Machine by Porter Robinson inspired by Didrick & Ember Island's cover of the song.

The first project I produced and finished. And, well, it shows. Notice how loud that snare is?
I have projects before this, and we don't talk about the work from 2020. Some are significantly better than others. None of them ever crossed the finish line.

This video took me way too long to render on my 2016 MacBook Pro, and I only caught that the snare was stupidly loud a week or so after I uploaded it. The preview in DaVinci Resolve wasn't running at the full framerate, which made it hard to gauge timing.
I should've at least fixed the audio, it would've just been taking the rendered video and replacing the audio tracks, but I didn't think of that at the time. I didn't want to re-render the video to fix the issues.
Sadly, I deleted some outdated plugins which means, so I can't re-render it with changes applied.
