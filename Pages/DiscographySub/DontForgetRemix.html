An orchestral remix of Toby Fox & Laura Shigihara's Don't Forget from Deltarune. Heavily inspired by Cami-Cat's cover of the song.

I wasn't sure if I wanted to upload this. It would've been an unlisted release but released it publicly due to time investment.
I started this tiny project in May 2023, but I didn't really like how it was going, so I abandoned it until the fall of 2023, and that's when I finished it with a revision.
I played Deltarune way too late, I slept on it! It's such a good game, which made for the final push to release it.

Project File!

You can find the Ableton Live project files here on Google Drive.
The revised version needs Live 12.1+, while the old one is on Live 11. Only samples are collected. Nothing is frozen or bounced to audio.
