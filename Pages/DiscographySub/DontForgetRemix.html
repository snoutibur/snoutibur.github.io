I actually wasn't sure if I wanted to upload this, I only recently considered putting it out as an unlisted video, but then I sunk too much time into the potato artwork, so it's a public release now!
I started this tiny project back in May 2023, but I didn't really like how it was going, and so I just abandoned it until 2023's fall, and that's when I finished it. Most recent change I made was just on a transition that wasn't the best.
I played Deltarune way too late, I definitely slept on it! It's such a good game. That's just like the final push, like dang it I'm not just gonna keep this sitting around in my finished work that won't make it out there.

Useless fact! I believe this was the only project I completed, start to finish in 2023. The Shelter remix was started in 2022, and basically finished that same year.
